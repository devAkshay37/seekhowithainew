# Features & Tools

SeekhoWithAI provides a suite of AI-powered tools designed specifically for school teachers.

## 1. TeachPack
The flagship tool that generates a full lesson content package.

### Content Generated:
- **Lesson Overview**: Concept summary, learning goals, and key ideas.
- **Learning Objectives**: Specific outcomes for students.
- **Teaching Flow**: Sequential phases (Introduction, Teaching, Activity, Assessment) with suggested durations.
- **Teacher Explanation**: Concepts breakdown, analogies, and real-life examples.
- **Classroom Questions**: Recall, understanding, application, and critical thinking questions.
- **Teacher Notes**: Detailed notes in English, Hindi, Marathi, or Gujarati.

### Features:
- **Add-ons**: Extend TeachPacks with Worksheets, Homework, Assessments, and extra analogies/examples.
- **Regenerate & Edit**: Each section is inline-editable and can be regenerated individually.
- **PDF Export**: Download "Teacher PDF" (full) or "Student PDF" (worksheet only).

---

## 2. Last Min Prep
Quickly prepare for class with glanceable revision cards.

### Features:
- **Depth Slider**: Choose between "Quick Glance" (1-2 min) and "Deep Refresh" (3-5 min).
- **Core Concept**: 2-3 sentence summary.
- **Common Confusions**: Highlight what students typically struggle with.
- **Best Analogy**: Single strongest analogy for the topic.

---

## 3. Mindmap Creator
Visualize concepts through interactive mindmaps.

### Features:
- **Interactive SVG**: Tap/click nodes to see details, pan and zoom.
- **Configurable Depth**: Broad Overview, Standard, or Detailed.
- **Attach to TeachPack**: Link your mindmap to its corresponding lesson package.

---

## 4. Quiz / Test Paper Creator
Generate printable test papers with automated mark distribution.

### Configuration:
- **Difficulty**: Easy, Medium, Hard, Mixed.
- **Question Types**: MCQ, Fill in the Blanks, Match the Following, Short Answer, Long Answer.
- **Automated Marks**: Proportional distribution across selected question types.
- **Answer Key**: Option to generate a teacher-facing answer key.

---

## 5. Activity Generator
Design classroom activities based on Social Emotional Learning (SEL) principles.

### Content:
- **SEL Framework**: Addressed competencies like social awareness and responsible decision-making.
- **Real-World Connection**: Relatable context for students.
- **Instructions**: Step-by-step teacher guide with role assignments for group activities.
- **Materials**: Locally available classroom items.
